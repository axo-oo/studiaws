Automatism

  • Hill v Baxter (1958): Automatism must be caused by an external factor, e.g., being attacked by bees.
  • R v T (1990): Exceptional stress from external trauma can trigger automatism.
  • AG’s Ref (No.2 of 1992): Partial loss of control is not sufficient for automatism.
